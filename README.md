# 👋 Cheers, I'm Krystal — Sports Analyst, Storyteller, & Stat Sheet Decoder

Welcome to my GitHub portfolio! I’m transitioning into the **sports analytics** field with a focus on data-driven storytelling across **women’s college basketball and softball, pro leagues like the WNBA, AUSL, and NWSL**, and the **sports betting / DFS industry**.

I combine experience in **sports media, data analysis, and storytelling** to create impactful dashboards, predictive models, and content that helps decode the game — one stat at a time.

## 🏀 What I’m Building

- 🎙️ **The Stat Sheet**: A content series (blog/video/dashboards) that breaks down one advanced stat at a time (like USG%, eFG%, or Offensive Rating), making it useful and actionable for new fans, bettors, and analysts.
- **Cal 2024-2025 Season Analysis**: NCAA D1 women's basketball
- **Golden State Valkyries Scouting Report**: inaugural season of WNBA's newest expansion team, the Golden State Valkyries!
- 📊 **NCAA Women’s Basketball + WNBA Draft Models**: Using historical data to predict draft outcomes, player performance, and conference success.
- ⚾ **Softball Bracket Breakdown Dashboards**: Visualizing the structure of NCAA D1 regionals, super regionals, and team paths to the WCWS.
- 🤖 **DFS-Focused ML Experiments**: Forecasting player prop bets and usage scenarios based on trends, opportunity metrics, and opponent stats.

## 🛠️ Tools & Skills
- **Languages & Libraries**: Python (Pandas, NumPy, Scikit-learn), SQL, Google Sheets
- **Data Viz & Dashboards**: Tableau Public, Looker Studio, Excel
- **Workflow & Storytelling**: GitHub, Notion, Canva
- **Key Strengths**: Data cleaning • Basketball analysis • Visualization • Betting logic • Sports modeling • Sports statsistics • Narrative framing
